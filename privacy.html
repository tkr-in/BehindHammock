Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2, 2025  |  Bobby's Dev Room

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how Bobby's Dev Room ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use BehindHammock (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect your name, email address, or account credentials, location data, device identifiers beyond what is described in Section 3, health or sleep data, payment information (all purchases are handled entirely by Apple), or any data you enter into the app such as tasks or notes.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of the App displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, subject to your ATT consent), device type and operating system version, and general location inferred from IP address.

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. When you first open the App, you will be asked for permission to track your data for advertising purposes via the App Tracking Transparency prompt on iOS. You may decline at any time.

To learn more about how Google uses data, visit policies.google.com/privacy.

Users with an active subscription do not see ads.

4. In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

All purchases and subscription billing are managed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. For billing questions or to cancel a subscription, visit your Apple ID settings or the Manage Subscriptions page in the App.

5. Local Data Storage

Some data such as your diamond balance, unlocked content, and subscription status is stored locally on your device using the device keychain or local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into the App is Google AdMob for advertising. We do not integrate any analytics platforms, social login providers, or data brokers.

8. Your Rights

Because we do not collect personal data, there is no data of yours for us to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about the advertising data collected by AdMob, you can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads in your iOS device settings under Privacy and Security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
